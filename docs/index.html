EasyDispatch

Realtime Field Service Dispatcher, in seconds

Unified Framework

Including three types of algorithms(Heuristic, Optimization, Reinforcement Learning) in one dispatching platform.

Flexible Business Rules

Rules plugins can be embedded into core engine to specify what is allowed and leave engine to decide how to reach a solution.

Explainable AI

Clear objective scoring and business rule visualization for each worker-job assignment candidates.
